About Me

Hello! My name is Justin and I'm a UW graduate student that majored in Science, Technology, and Society. While minored in Computer Science and Software Engineering. I have always had a strong passion for technology and problem-solving, which has shaped my educational and career goals. My aim is to become a successful software engineer, building innovative programs and solutions that will have a significant impact on both the business world and the people who use them. I strive to excel in my academic pursuits and constantly challenge myself to reach new heights, both personally and professionally. One of my core motivations is to make a living that not only sustains me but also allows me to give back to my family, who have supported me throughout my journey.

My academic accomplishments include making the Dean's List at the University of Washington Bothell, where I have maintained an above-average GPA of 3.6. This recognition is a testament to my commitment to excellence and my determination to succeed. The journey to this point has required a lot of hard work, discipline, and time management, especially as I balanced my academic responsibilities with other activities and work experiences. This recognition has only motivated me further to push the limits of what I can achieve and contribute to the tech world.

Looking ahead, I am excited about the future and the opportunities that lie before me in the field of software engineering. I know that my past experiences, both my successes and the challenges I’ve faced, have equipped me with the skills and mindset necessary to achieve my goals. Whether it’s pushing through difficult coursework, working on complex projects, or helping others through leadership roles, I am committed to continuously learning, improving, and making a positive impact in whatever I do. My journey is far from over, and I am confident that with determination, hard work, and a desire to make a difference, I will be able to achieve my goals and give back to the people and communities that have supported me along the way.
